Meet the team.

Renuka Prabhakar
Founder/Interim CEO

Renuka will receive her B.S.M.E. from the University of Washington in December of 2010. Her return to UW in 2007 was motivated by her passion for the environment and her desire to develop sustainable technologies. She brings over 15 years of experience in small business management and light manufacturing. Renuka is an entrepreneur at heart and started her first company in 1996, when she was just 22 years of age. Since her return to UW, Renuka secured a prestigious position as a NASA Co-op, received the 2010 SWE “Outstanding Female” Award, and was nominated for the 2010 UW Community of Innovators Award.

Grant L.S. Marchelli
Founder/CTO

Grant is a Ph.D. track M.S. Candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at UW, a Xerox Technical Graduate Fellow, and a certified EIT in the State of Washington. His thesis topic encompasses the mechanical characterization of porous ceramic and glass materials created using both conventional and rapid manufacturing techniques. Grant has devoted over 14 months of intensive research into characterizing ceramic and glass behavior and has been regarded as one of the World's leading experts in 3DP porous glass.
