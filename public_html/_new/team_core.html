core team
Renuka Prabhakar President & CEO
Ms. Prabhakar is EnVitrum’s President and Chief Executive Officer. She has over 15 years of experience in operations management within the manufacturing and repair industries, including 6 years of experience as a small business owner. Ms. Prabhakar’s expertise is in process optimization and quality assurance. Throughout her career, Ms. Prabhakar has successfully developed and implemented innovative manufacturing strategies for new products. She has played significant roles in product development and design. Ms. Prabhakar will receive her MSME from the University of Washington in 2012. She is a NASA Civil Servant Graduate Co-op at the Johnson Space Center, where her focus is the research and development of advanced materials and manufacturing processes. In addition, she received the 2010 Society of Women Engineers “Outstanding Female” Award, and was nominated for the 2010 UW Community of Innovators Award.
Grant Marchelli E.I.T., M.S. - CTO
Mr. Marchelli is EnVitrum’s Chief Technical Officer. Mr. Marchelli holds his MSME from the University of Washington and expects to receive his Ph.D. in early 2013. He is a two-time Xerox Technical Minority Graduate Fellow, and a certified EIT in the State of Washington. Over the past three years, his focus has been on the development and mechanical characterization of ceramic and glass materials. He has extensive experience with testing and analysis of vitreous materials in accordance with ASTM standards, Mr. Marchelli has developed a number of mathematical models for new material systems and is the lead author on a number of high-impact journal publications. His most recent paper on glass was chosen as an “Outstanding Paper” at an international conference and subsequently published in the special edition of a leading international journal.
