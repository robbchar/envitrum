advisory board - corporate advisors EnVitrum draws knowledge from an experienced and well-rounded advisory board consisting of individuals with expertise in all relevant disciplines.
Carsten Stinn LEED AP BD+C — Perkins+Will Carsten is a sustainable project design architect at the global firm Perkins+Will. Carsten is a LEED Accredited Professional in Building Design and Construction and an AIA member. As a senior designer, he has spearheaded numerous sustainable projects throughout the region.
Jim Starkey VP of Engineering — Starkey Machinery For over 30 years, Jim has designed and manufactured process equipment for the masonry industry. His company focuses on extrusion techniques and equipment. Jim’s experience ranges from perfecting lab-scale processes to outfitting full scale plants for major manufacturers.
Marc Holmes Director, Sales and Marketing — Greencast Marc has over 25 years of experience in the masonry industry and has held leadership positions in several prominent companies. His primary expertise is in sales and marketing in both regional and national markets. Marc has extensive experience in operations management and production.
Ross Wildman Former Executive Director — Masonry Institute of Washington Ross joined the MIW in 2009 and was responsible for establishing the overall direction of the organization. MIW’s mission is to promote the use of masonry and much of Ross’s efforts were focused on outreach within both the construction and architectural communities. Ross also oversaw the MIW training facility, which is a vital industry resource. He is now a Marketing Manager at BNBuilders.
Jeff Freas Co-Founder and VP of Marketing — TriVitro Jeff is one of the founding members of TriVitro, a leader in sustainable glass processing and manufacturing. TriVitro provides high quality products that divert material from the waste stream. Jeff specializes in branding and channel strategies for new product lines. He has led the marketing efforts for several of TriVitro’s most successful products.
Lars Johansson Cleantech Investor — Northwest Energy Angels Lars is an active cleantech angel investor and co-chair of the Northwest Energy Angels. He is an Entrepreneur-in-Residence at UW C4C, where his primary focus is commercialization opportunities in the cleantech space. Lars has 16 years of executive level experience at Microsoft.
